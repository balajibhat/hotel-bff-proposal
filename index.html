Global Regulatory Landscape

The time is now.

Governments worldwide are mandating sustainability for hotels — not suggesting it. Non-compliance means fines, permit denials, and lost market access. These are confirmed regulations with real enforcement.

European Union
Sep 2026

Green Transition Directive bans unsubstantiated sustainability claims. Hotels using "eco-friendly" or "green" without third-party certification face fines up to 4% of annual turnover.

Directive (EU) 2024/825
European Union
2030

Energy Performance of Buildings Directive requires worst-performing buildings to meet minimum standards. Non-compliant hotels can be declared unusable or denied operating licenses.

Directive (EU) 2024/1275
India
Active Now

Energy Conservation Building Code mandatory in 23 of 28 states. Building permits denied without compliance. Energy Conservation Act 2022 mandates audits with fines up to INR 10 lakh.

ECBC 2017 + EC Act 2022
Dubai, UAE
Active Now

Al Sa'fat Green Building System requires minimum Bronze rating for all new hotels. Building permits denied until compliance proven. Fines up to AED 2,000,000 (~$545K).

Dubai Municipality
Japan
April 2025

All new buildings — including hotels — must meet energy-saving standards. No floor area minimum. Fines up to JPY 3,000,000 (~$20K). ZEH standards required by 2030.

Building Energy Conservation Act 2022
Australia
Mid-2026

NCC 2025 requires mandatory on-site solar PV for hotels. 4-star NABERS Energy rating minimum. Commercial Building Disclosure fines up to AUD 330,000 per breach.

National Construction Code 2025

Singapore, South Korea, Thailand, and New Zealand also have mandatory regulations in force or taking effect by 2026. The window to get ahead is closing.

Attribution

Clean attribution. No guesswork.

Why a microsite?

A dedicated paid media microsite keeps organic and paid traffic completely separate. Every lead that comes through the microsite is a direct result of CDE's campaigns — no ambiguity, no overlap.

  • ✓ 100% attribution on every lead
  • ✓ Per-channel optimization without interference
  • ✓ Isolated analytics for clear reporting
  • ✓ A/B testing without affecting the main site

What counts as a qualified lead?

Not every form fill is a qualified lead. Both teams review leads monthly to ensure quality stays high and billing stays fair.

  • ✓ Must be an operating hotel
  • ✓ Located in an eligible market
  • ✓ Filled out the lead form on the microsite
  • ✓ Valid contact information provided
